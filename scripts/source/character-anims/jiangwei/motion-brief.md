## Motion Grammar
- idle: Calm strategist guard: spear hand, scroll hand, ponytail, sleeves, and tassel each shift subtly across six frames.
  - layers: hand, weapon, hair, sleeve
  - effect: tiny spear-tip glints from spear tassel along short drifting orbit near the spear head (soft alternating glints)
- run: Light tactical stride: feet alternate clearly, body leans into the run, spear trails behind then catches up, ponytail and sash counter-swing.
  - layers: feet, weapon, hair, sash
  - effect: teal step motes from feet and spear butt along short backward dots that rise with each stride (alternating footfall)
- attack: Eight-frame ordinary attack: ready guard, spear lowers, shoulder pulls back, forked blade hooks diagonally, forward thrust lands, tassel follows through, weapon recoils, stance resets.
  - layers: arm, feet, weapon, tassel
  - effect: split crescent spear wake from forked blade tip along up-left hook into short forward thrust with an inner echo line (wind-up, hook, stab, settle)
- ultimate: Eight-frame Musou: command stance with scroll, spear gathers into a compact angled path, body drives forward, heavy finishing thrust, cape and ponytail settle last.
  - layers: torso, feet, weapon, cape
  - effect: open angled qilin star shards from spear head and military scroll along open diagonal star path that folds into a final thrust (charge, star burst, forward finish)
